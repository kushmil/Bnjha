Our History

Established in the heart of Deoghar, BN Jha Memorial Inter College has been a beacon of knowledge and discipline for decades. Named in memory of the visionary Late B.N. Jha, our institution aims to uplift the educational standards of the region.

We offer higher secondary education in Science, Arts, and Commerce, guided by a team of dedicated faculties who believe in holistic development and shaping the leaders of tomorrow.

Core Values

Vision

To be an institution of excellence, nurturing intellect, fostering values, and empowering students to contribute to society.

Mission

Providing quality education, modern infrastructure, and an inclusive environment for all-round personality development.

Discipline

Maintaining a strict yet supportive environment that builds character and professional ethics in students.

Inclusivity

Ensuring education is accessible to all, promoting unity in diversity across the campus.
